# VillaBaas
### Luxury Villa Booking Platform for Nepal

**VillaBaas** (Villa + Baas — "stay" in Nepali) is a full-stack villa rental and booking platform designed specifically for the Nepali hospitality market. It bridges the gap between Nepal's growing tourism industry and the lack of a centralized, modern booking experience.

Guests can discover curated luxury villas across Nepal's most breathtaking destinations — from Pokhara's lakeside retreats to Nagarkot's mountain escapes and Chitwan's jungle estates — with secure authentication, personalized dashboards, and a seamless booking flow, all without the friction of third-party travel agents or outdated listing sites.

VillaBaas modernizes Nepal's villa rental market by connecting property owners directly with travelers, giving guests the premium booking experience they deserve — entirely built and tailored for Nepal.
